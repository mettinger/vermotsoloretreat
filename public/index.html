Purpose

We provide a secluded, quiet environment for practitioners to pursue a solo meditation and spiritual practice. ‘Solo’ means there is no resident teacher and the practitioner is entirely responsible for his/her schedule of practice. We believe that most retreat centers are far too expensive. Our cost is only $40 per person per day. You may use the fully equiped kitchen to prepare your own meals or opt to have meals provided for an extra $50 per person per day. Please note this retreat is a place for spiritual practice and philosophical reflection and is not intended for use as a vacation destination.

Accomodations

The retreat house is a secluded 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom, 3 story, 2500 square foot house nestled in the forests of Dorset, Vermont. The house has a fully equipped kitchen and internet access. We can accomodate as many as four practitioners (one couple per bedroom).
